## Video encoding

Youtube recommendations: <https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/1722171?hl=en>

* Container: MP4
* Audio codec: AAC-LC
* Video codec: H.264
* Frame rate: 60fps (maybe 30)
* Bitrate: 12 Mbps (8 Mbps for 30fps)
* Resolution and aspect ratio: 16:9  (maybe 1920x1080)
